Event Information

Accessibility

We aim to make all events accessible. Most walks involve uneven terrain. Contact us in advance to discuss specific accessibility requirements.

What to Bring

Sturdy footwear, weather-appropriate clothing, water, and sun protection. For volunteer days, bring gloves and tools if you have them (we also provide these).

Family Friendly

Children are welcome at most events when accompanied by adults. Some walks and talks may be more suitable for older children and adults.

Weather Cancellations

Events proceed in most weather conditions unless unsafe. Check our Facebook group or email hello@farthingdowns.org.uk on the day if in doubt.
