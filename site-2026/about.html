About the Friends

Protecting ancient landscapes since 2005

Who We Are

Friends of Farthing Downs & Happy Valley is a volunteer group dedicated to protecting and promoting these ancient chalk downlands for everyone to enjoy.

Our Mission

We work in partnership with the City of London Corporation to:

  • Preserve the unique chalk grassland habitat and its rare species
  • Protect the archaeological features and historical character
  • Promote understanding and enjoyment of these special places
  • Engage the local community in conservation and education
  • Support practical conservation work through volunteers

What We Do

Conservation Projects

The "Hillbillies" volunteer group meets monthly for hands-on conservation work - from scrub clearance to dormouse box installation.

Community Events

Guided walks, talks, wildlife surveys, and our Annual General Meeting with guest speakers connect people with the Downs.

Our Committee

The Friends are run by a volunteer committee elected annually at the AGM. We're always looking for new committee members - if you'd like to get involved, please get in touch.

Current Roles

  • Chair - Leads the committee and represents the Friends
  • Treasurer - Manages finances and membership records
  • Secretary - Coordinates meetings and communications
  • Volunteer Coordinator - Organizes the Hillbillies conservation group
  • Events Officer - Plans walks, talks, and activities
  • Committee Members - Support all aspects of the Friends' work

Join the Committee

We welcome new committee members with skills in fundraising, communications, events, conservation, or simply enthusiasm for the Downs. Committee meetings are held quarterly, with additional email correspondence between meetings.

Privacy Notice

How We Use Your Information

Friends of Farthing Downs & Happy Valley is committed to protecting your privacy. This notice explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

What We Collect

  • Name and email address (for membership communications)
  • Postal address (if you request printed newsletters)
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe - we don't store card details)

How We Use It

  • To send you membership communications (newsletters, event invites, AGM notices)
  • To process membership payments and donations
  • To fulfill any services you've requested (printed materials, etc.)

Your Rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete your data
  • Unsubscribe from communications at any time
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

We will never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. We retain member data only for as long as you're an active member, plus 2 years for Gift Aid purposes if applicable.

Questions? Contact us at hello@farthingdowns.org.uk
