## Important Safety Notes

**Please note:** Fungi are very perishable, their tissues contain over 90% water, and bruise and decay quickly.

**Never eat (even) a proven edible species once it is past its firm best!**

### Poisonous Species Found (marked P)
1. *Agaricus xanthodermus* - Yellow staining mushroom
2. *Hebeloma crustuliniforme* - Poison pie
3. *Hypholoma fasciculare* - Sulphur tuft
4. *Inocybe lilacina*
5. *Mycena pura* - Lilac mycena
