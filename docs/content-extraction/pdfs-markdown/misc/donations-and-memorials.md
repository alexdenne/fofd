## How to Donate

The Friends of Farthing Downs & Happy Valley welcome donations of all sizes. Funds are used for:

- Conservation equipment and materials
- Wildlife habitat improvements (dormouse boxes, bird feeders)
- Trail maintenance and signage
- Educational materials and nature trail resources
- Community facilities (benches, defibrillators)

**Contact the Chairman or Secretary to discuss how your donation can support our work.**
