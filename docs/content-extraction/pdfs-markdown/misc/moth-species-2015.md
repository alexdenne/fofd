**Summary:** This document records the species observed during a moth night event at Farthing Downs. The survey identified 23 macro moth species, 5 micro moth species, and several other nocturnal creatures including caddis flies, an ichneumon wasp, glow-worms, and pipistrelle bats. The event demonstrates the rich biodiversity of nocturnal wildlife at Farthing Downs.
