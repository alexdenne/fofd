# Farthing Downs Archaeology Day

**Saturday, 2nd September, from 10:30 to 3:30 p.m.**

## CELEBRATING 75 Years since...
### ...the Excavations of Brian Hope-Taylor

An RAF Officer at Kenley, B.H-T became interested in Farthing Downs. He returned after the war to uncover its ancient history.

## What to See and Do

**History Walks** 11.30 and 2 p.m. Led by Jane Sidell of Historic England

**Talk** 1.30 p.m. on B H-T aylor by Andrew Carver, FoFD&HV

**Display of Archaeological Artifacts** - Chris Taylor, CNHSS

**Exhibitions:** City of London : Bourne Society : FoFD&HV
