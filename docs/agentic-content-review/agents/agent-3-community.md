## Research Quick Reference

### RSPB TikTok Success (from research)
- 190% follower growth by hiring young creator with creative freedom
- "Bird of the Week" series with Gen Z cultural references
- 80% of 16-24 year olds agree nature benefits mental health

### Chiltern Rangers Model
- Simple Google Form for registration
- "Green Thursday" low-commitment entry point
- Clear guidelines for all ages
- Variety of role types

### Four Engagement Pillars
1. **Authentic Content**: Expert-led blogs, ranger updates (Paxton Pits model)
2. **Interactive**: Naming contests, photo competitions (High Park Zoo)
3. **Personality**: Embrace viral moments, character-driven stories
4. **Partnerships**: Co-host events, cross-promote with partners
