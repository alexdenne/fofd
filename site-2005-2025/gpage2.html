SUNDAY, 15th JUNE, 10.30 a.m. – 3.30 p.m.

 

Celebrating 75 years since the Site of Special Scientific Interest (SSSI) designation of Farthing Downs & Happy Valley
Join the Friends, City of London Rangers, Happy Valley Countryside Warden and RSPA.
Croydon Natural History & Scientific Society, Bourne Society and other Volunteer Groups will be running stalls.
The day also marks 25 years since the unveiling of our Millennium Cairn and 20 years since the inauguration of Friends of Farthing Downs & Happy Valley.
Enjoy walks, talks and stalls. Details nearer the time.
Contact: email pauline@payne42.co.uk or phone 01737 554449.

SATURDAY, 12th JULY

 

Old Coulsdon Village Fair: Grange Park, Old Coulsdon, 12 to 4.30 p.m.
Visit the Friends stall for our exhibition, information and TOMBOLA.
Funds raised will support Friends of Farthing Downs & Happy Valley projects.
For further information, please visit Rotary Club of Coulsdon Manor website.

SATURDAY, 19th July

 

Farthing Downs Summer Butterfly Walk
Meet Ian Pratley and the Friends in Farthing Downs Car Park at 1.30 p.m.
Ian will lead this 1½ hour gentle walk to look at the rare butterflies here.
Time permitting, Ian will take those interested to the fields in Happy Valley.
Please wear suitable clothing and bring a bottle of water if it is hot.
To book (ESSENTIAL): email: pauline@payne42.co.uk or phone 01737 554449.
