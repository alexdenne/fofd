Who can become a Member

Membership is open to any individual or family who wishes to support Friends of Farthing Downs and Happy Valley in protecting our heritage and environment, supporting community projects which enhance the appeal of Farthing Downs and Happy Valley and working with the City of London (Farthing Downs) and Croydon Council (Happy Valley) on conservation projects to further these aims.

Regular Activities

Organising nature and history walks and talks and exhibitions.

Stalls at local events.
Greening of Coulsdon South Station: maintaining flower-beds and flower boxes.

Half-yearly quizzes to support fundraising.

 

Benefits of Membership

You will be supporting a local group which is dedicated to preserving historic Farthing Downs as an Ancient Scheduled Monument and Farthing Downs and Happy Valley as Sites of Special Scientific Interest.

You will have preferential invites to all our activities.
You will be welcome to join our projects and make a difference to our green environment.

You will receive our half-yearly newsletter keeping you up to date with what’s going on.

General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR)



The Friends implement their obligations under the GDPR by issuing the following Data Privacy Statement:

Friends of Farthing Downs & Happy Valley (FoFD&HV – the Friends)
Data Privacy Statement – September 2018

The Friends hold the following data on its Members: Name, Address, Telephone Number(s), Email Address, Membership type, Subscription record.

Only Committee Members, who require access to this personal data needed to carry out their role within the Friends, are provided with access to the data. This includes mailing of newsletters, leaflets and events information, maintaining membership and subscription records and contact details for finding volunteers for specific purposes.

Information will not be shared with third parties without express permission from the member concerned.

END
