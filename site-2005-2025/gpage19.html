Welcome to the Happy Valley and Farthing Downs Nature Trail. The trail can be followed in two ways:

• Audio trail: Spoken in English and narrated by well-known personalities – actors, musicians, poets, broadcasters, writers and artists. The audio trail is also available in several other languages. Please select from those below if you wish:
• Text trail: This can be followed as an alternative to the audio trail, and has photographs, illustrations and maps to help you find your way around.

Please choose the desired post number and select either the audio or text version from below. Use the back arrow on your phone or browser to return to this screen.

We hope you enjoy Walking the Happy Valley and Farthing Downs Nature Trail!
